All about Narrabundah College library facility

The Narrabundah College Library Catalogue is currently only available for viewing from inside the College.

ACT Libraries

ACT Libraries eResources (http://www.library.act.gov.au/eresources)

To search another library, try the ACT Public Library catalogue at ACT Government Library (www.library.act.gov.au/find/search). You can find and reserve resources online.

This website also has access to excellent full text online databases where you can read journal and newspaper articles in many subject areas. You will need to type in your library card number and PIN number. Go to ACT Libraries eResources (http://www.library.act.gov.au/eresources)

You can also search the ANU Library at ANU Library (http://library.anu.edu.au).

Members of the public are welcome to visit the ANU Library to study and browse. You may borrow from the library for a fee.

There are also links to other university library catalogues and a link to the Libraries Australia website.

You can also search the online catalogue at the National Library of Australia (www.nla.gov.au).

You cannot borrow from the NLA but you can use the reading rooms.
