All about Narrabundah College canteen facility

Narrabundah College has spacious, clean and big canteen facility in the Narrabundah College. A professional hand's tasty and delecious food and snack items are provided with highest quality.

Narrabundah College canteen has a big kitchen and kitchen staff take extra care with nutritious and hygienic food in the Narrabundah College campus canteen. Food items are prepared with RO treated water.

It also provides light refreshment like packed snack items, cakes, veg. puff, beverages etc to the students and staff of the Narrabundah College.

Narrabundah College canteen is open from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM on all working days.
